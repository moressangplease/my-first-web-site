Guardians of Galaxy


The Guardians of Galaxy is a 2014 American superhero film based on the Marvel Comics superhero team of the same name. Produced by Marvel Studios and distributed by Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures, it is the 10th film in the Marvel Cinematic Universe(MCU).

Directed by James Gunn, who wrote the screenplay with Nicole Perlman.
